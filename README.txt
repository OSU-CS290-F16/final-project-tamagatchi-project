# Final_Project
The Final Project for CS-290: a website that features a mock Tamagotchi game that stores the log data in the cookies on the computer that uses it. Take care of your Tamagotchi pet by training it, feeding it, playing with your pet, and more! View your pet's stats to make check the hunger, happiness, training and age levels. Over time, your pet will grow old and your pet's appearenes will change based on age. Make sure to take great care of your pet!
